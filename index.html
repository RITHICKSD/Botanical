Botany Lab

The Future of DNA Preservation

Our state-of-the-art laboratory works tirelessly to map the genomes of endangered species, ensuring that even if habitats are lost, the genetic legacy survives. Through advanced cryo-preservation and digital gene-mapping, we provide a biological safety net for thousands of flora types currently facing extinction.
